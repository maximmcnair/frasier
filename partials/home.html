Talllies uses psychological observations to help you work faster.

Parkison’s Law

This observation dictates a task will swell in complexity to the time allotted for its completion, we push you to create short and imminent deadlines for each task. This forces us to ignore minutia and focus on the important.

Taking Breaks & The Ultradian Rhythm

The basic understanding is that our human minds can focus on any given task for 90-120 minutes. Afterwards, a 20-30 minute break is required for us to get the renewal to achieve high performance for our next task again.
